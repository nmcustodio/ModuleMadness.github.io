Instructions

  1. Before starting the game, a recommendation is to check the “Tips & Tricks” section for more information about the proper typing position to achieve a faster typing speed.
  2. Press "Play" if you want to commence the game
  3. As fast as you can, type the word approaching the character to avoid losing heart points. There are a total of 3 life points so, watch out!
  4. Every correct word typed lits up to a color green. A wrongly-typed term will show a red color indicating that it is incorrect or different from what is being asked.
  5. Each word that the person failed to type on time and hits the student-character consequences into a decrement of one heart.
